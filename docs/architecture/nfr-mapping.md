# NFR Mapping

Maps non-functional requirements (NFRs) from upstream artefacts to architecture concerns.

---

## Security
- Enforce ISO/IEC 27001 controls, RBAC, SSO
- Data encryption in transit and at rest

## Privacy
- GDPR, ISO/IEC 27701 compliance
- Data minimization, subject rights, audit logging

## Performance
- Timely posture computation, dashboard responsiveness

## Availability
- High uptime, disaster recovery

## Audit
- Evidence retention, audit logging, regular reviews

## Change Management
- CAB approval, traceability, anti-drift contract
