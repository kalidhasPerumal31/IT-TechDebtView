# Decision Backlog

All decisions required for downstream implementation, architecture, or compliance, as identified in Phases 1–2.

---

## Decision-Required
- Policy for “Limited/Extended Support”: Should this be treated as Green (In Support) or Amber?
- IBS source of truth: Is IBS mastered in ServiceNow or in this application?
- Type granularity: Application-level only, or allow component overrides for hybrids?
- EoL provider prioritization: Which vendor families are prioritized for initial implementation?
- Alert thresholds and escalation: Confirm 180/90/60/30 days and IBS elevated routing.
- Manual override process: How are urgent EoL mapping overrides handled?
- Handling of “Unknown” status: What is the escalation/reporting policy?
