# Interpretation Contract

This contract defines the canonical interpretation of all requirements, constraints, and decisions for Tech Debt Posture & Lifecycle Manager, per AG-REQ-01.

---

## Canonical Interpretation
- All requirements, ADRs, and compliance artefacts are binding unless explicitly marked as optional or decision-required.
- No downstream agent may weaken, reinterpret, or override these outputs without an approved ADR and explicit HITL approval.
- All compliance anchors must be enforced in downstream design and implementation.
- RTM traceability must be maintained at all times.

## Change Control
- Any change to binding requirements or compliance artefacts must be escalated via ADR + HITL.
- This contract is binding for all downstream agents and human participants.
