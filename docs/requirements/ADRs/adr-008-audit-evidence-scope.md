# ADR-008: Audit Evidence Scope

## Status
Binding

## Context
Audit-ready evidence must be retained and available for compliance and regulatory review.

## Decision
All compliance artefacts (logs, reports, risk registers) are retained and available for audit. Compliance mapping is updated annually or upon regulatory change.

## Consequences
- Increased operational overhead if evidence scope is too broad.
- Ensures audit and compliance readiness.
