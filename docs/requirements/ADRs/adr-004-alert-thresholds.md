# ADR-004: Alert Thresholds & Escalation

## Status
Decision-Required

## Context
Alert thresholds (180/90/60/30 days) and IBS elevated routing need confirmation for business fit.

## Decision
Adopt 180/90/60/30 day alert thresholds and implement IBS-elevated routing for critical services.

## Consequences
- Standard thresholds: Familiar, but may need tuning.
- IBS elevated: Ensures critical services get attention, but may increase noise.
