# Risk Register

This register documents risks identified during requirements engineering, ambiguity/conflict analysis, and compliance mapping.

---

## Risks Identified
- Ambiguity in “Limited/Extended Support” policy may lead to inconsistent risk posture.
- IBS source of truth not fixed may cause data drift or governance issues.
- Type granularity (application vs. component) may cause reporting and policy inconsistencies.
- Alert thresholds and escalation policy may not fit all business needs if not tailored.
- EoL provider prioritization may miss niche risks initially.
- Manual override process for EoL mappings may slow urgent remediation if not streamlined.
- Handling of “Unknown” status may mask risk if not escalated appropriately.
- Audit-ready evidence scope may increase operational overhead if too comprehensive.
- Compliance with all listed standards must be maintained; any deviation is a risk.
