# Privacy Requirements

This document details privacy requirements for Tech Debt Posture & Lifecycle Manager, per AG-REQ-01 and compliance anchors.

---

## Data Minimization
- Only necessary data is collected and processed for lifecycle management and risk posture analysis.

## Purpose Limitation
- Data is used solely for lifecycle management, risk posture analysis, and compliance reporting.

## Data Subject Rights
- Data subjects can request access, rectification, and erasure of their data.
- Requests are fulfilled within statutory timelines (GDPR, ISO/IEC 27701).

## Privacy Impact Assessment
- All personal data processing is subject to privacy impact assessment.
- Privacy risks are documented in the risk register and mitigated as required.

## Data Retention and Disposal
- Data is retained for a minimum of 7 years to meet audit and regulatory requirements.
- Secure erasure procedures are followed for data disposal, with audit logging.

## Security Controls
- Data in transit and at rest is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
- Access is restricted via Entra ID SSO and RBAC.

## Audit and Monitoring
- All user and system activities are logged and retained for at least 12 months.
- Regular audits are conducted to ensure compliance with privacy requirements.
